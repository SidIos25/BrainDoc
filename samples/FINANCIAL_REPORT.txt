RISKS AND CHALLENGES

1. Market Competition
   - Increasing competition from larger cloud providers (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
   - Pricing pressure in commoditized segments

2. Customer Concentration
   - Top 10 customers represent approximately 28% of annual revenue
   - Loss of a major customer could impact results

3. Technology and Talent
   - Rapid technology changes require continuous R&D investment
   - High demand for skilled software engineers drives wage inflation

4. Regulatory and Compliance
   - Increasing data privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA) require compliance costs
   - Potential government restrictions on cloud services in certain regions

5. Foreign Exchange
   - 35% of revenue from international operations
   - Currency fluctuations can impact financial results

6. Economic Headwinds
   - Potential economic recession could reduce enterprise IT spending
   - Extended sales cycles may lengthen in downturn

MANAGEMENT'S OUTLOOK FOR 2026

- Revenue growth of 12-15% year-over-year
- Operating margin improvement to 21-22%
- EPS growth of 10-14%
- Continued investment in AI-powered features
- Potential strategic acquisitions
